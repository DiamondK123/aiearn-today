Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 2025
Summary: AIEarn.today participates in affiliate programs. When you click certain links on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you. This helps us keep the site free and updated daily.

What Is an Affiliate Link?

An affiliate link is a special tracked URL. When you click it and complete a purchase or sign up for a service, the company pays us a small commission as a referral fee. You pay exactly the same price — sometimes even less, if we've negotiated a discount for our readers.

Our Commitment to You

We only recommend products and services we believe are genuinely useful for people trying to make money with AI. Our editorial opinions are not influenced by affiliate relationships — if a tool isn't good, we'll say so regardless of whether we have an affiliate deal.

  • We clearly label affiliate links with an "Affiliate" tag wherever possible
  • We never recommend a product solely because it pays a high commission
  • Our reviews and analysis are based on actual product evaluation and user feedback
  • We disclose all material affiliate relationships on this page
