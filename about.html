About AIEarn.today

AIEarn.today is a daily-updated publication focused on one question: how can everyday people use AI tools to earn more money?

We launched because we noticed a gap. There were plenty of sites covering AI from a technical or investor perspective — but very few that answered the practical question most people actually have: "I've heard about these AI tools. How do I actually use them to make money?"

Our mission is simple: every morning, we publish curated news, original analysis, and actionable guides on making money with AI — whether you're a freelancer, a side hustler, a small business owner, or just someone looking for a new income stream.

Our Editorial Approach

We combine automated news curation with original editorial analysis. Our system monitors dozens of RSS feeds from leading AI and tech publications daily, then our editorial process selects and contextualizes the most relevant stories through the lens of practical income opportunities.
